3. Service description

LocalClip lets you sync clipboard text, screenshots, images and files between an Android phone and a Windows PC over your local Wi-Fi network, and text and screenshots over an encrypted Bluetooth connection when both devices support it.

It also includes LocalClip Board, an Android keyboard with clipboard integration, suggestions and productivity tools.
