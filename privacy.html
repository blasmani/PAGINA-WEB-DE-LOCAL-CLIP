2. Scope

This policy covers the LocalClip Windows app, the LocalClip Android app and this website.

3. Information LocalClip may process

LocalClip processes the content you choose to sync — clipboard text, screenshots and files — together with basic device information needed to pair and connect your phone and PC on the same network.

4. Purposes

Data is used only to enable synchronization between your devices, to display your transfer history locally and to operate the app's core features.

5. Consent

By installing and using LocalClip you consent to this processing. You can stop it at any time by closing the app or uninstalling it.

6. Local storage

History, settings and received files are stored locally on your devices. LocalClip does not require a cloud account to function.

7. Transfer between devices

Content is transferred directly between your phone and PC over your local network when both devices are paired.

8. Third-party services

The app may use third-party services for advertising or distribution (such as app stores). Those services have their own privacy policies.

9. Advertising and purchases

LocalClip may show ads and offer an option to remove them. Payment processing is handled by the corresponding store.

10. Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect transfers on your local network. No method of transmission is completely secure.

11. Retention and deletion

You control your local data and can delete history or files at any time from the app or your device.

12. Minors

LocalClip is not directed to children under the age required by your local law.

13. Your rights
