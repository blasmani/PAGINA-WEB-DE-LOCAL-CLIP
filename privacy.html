Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who is responsible

LocalClip is developed by Blasmani V. Titi Q. For any privacy request or question about this policy you can contact webbusinesstoolspro@gmail.com.

2. Scope

This policy covers the LocalClip Windows app, the LocalClip Android app (including the LocalClip Board keyboard) and this website. It explains what information the apps handle, where it is stored and what never leaves your devices.

3. Our privacy model: local first

LocalClip was designed so that your content travels directly between your own devices. There is no LocalClip cloud, no LocalClip account and no LocalClip server that receives your clipboard, screenshots or files. Synchronization happens over your local Wi-Fi network or over a direct Bluetooth connection between your phone and your PC.

4. Information LocalClip processes

Content you choose to sync: clipboard text, screenshots, images and files. This content is processed only to deliver it to your other device and to show it in your local history.

Pairing and connection data: device names, internal device identifiers generated by LocalClip, and the pairing credentials created when you scan the QR code. These exist so that only the devices you explicitly linked can talk to each other.

Basic technical data on your devices, such as settings and transfer history, stored locally so the app can work.

5. Information LocalClip does NOT collect

We do not read, upload, sell or share the content you sync. We do not build profiles from your clipboard or files. Your synchronized content is never sent to the developer or to any LocalClip server, because none exists.

6. Transfers between your devices

Over Wi-Fi, content travels through your local network using an encrypted channel (AES-GCM) after both devices are paired with the QR code.

Over Bluetooth, text and screenshots travel through an encrypted Bluetooth Low Energy channel (AES-GCM with per-session keys) established after an authenticated handshake between your paired devices.

In both modes the content goes device-to-device. It does not pass through the internet or through third-party servers.

7. The LocalClip Board keyboard

The keyboard runs entirely on your device. What you type is delivered to the app you are typing in, and is not logged or transmitted by LocalClip. Fields marked as sensitive (such as passwords) disable suggestions and clipboard offers.

8. Local storage

History, settings, pairing credentials and received files are stored locally on your devices. Sensitive credentials are protected with Android Keystore on Android and with Windows protected storage on Windows where applicable. You can delete history and files at any time from the app.

9. Permissions the apps request

Android may request: local network access (Wi-Fi sync and discovery), Bluetooth (nearby devices) for Bluetooth mode, notifications for transfer status, and access to the images you choose to share. The Bluetooth scan permission is declared as never used for location.

Windows may request: local network capability and Bluetooth capability for the same purposes. Each permission is used only for the feature it enables.

10. Advertising and purchases

The Android app may show ads served by Google AdMob and may offer an optional purchase to remove them. AdMob may process advertising identifiers under Google's own privacy policy. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play or Microsoft Store; LocalClip never sees your payment details.

11. Third-party services

Distribution is handled by Google Play and Microsoft Store, and advertising by Google AdMob. These services have their own privacy policies that apply to the data they process.

12. Security

Transfers are encrypted end-to-end between your paired devices, content integrity is verified with cryptographic hashes, and pairing requires physical access to both devices (scanning the QR). No method of transmission is completely secure, but LocalClip's local-only design keeps your content off the internet by default.

13. Retention and deletion

You control your data. Deleting an item from history, unpairing a device or uninstalling the app removes the corresponding local data. We cannot delete data for you because we never receive it.

14. Minors

LocalClip is not directed to children under the age required by your local law.

15. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights of access, correction, deletion, portability and objection. Because your data lives on your own devices, you can exercise most of them directly in the app. For anything else, write to webbusinesstoolspro@gmail.com and we will respond as soon as possible.

16. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the apps gain new features. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new revision date.
